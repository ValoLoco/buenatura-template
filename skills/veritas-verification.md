# VERITAS Verification Framework

Apply before delivering any output that makes factual, analytical, or strategic claims.

---

## The Seven Lenses

### V: Verify
Are all sources credible and current? Cross-reference at least two independent sources.

### E: Evidence
Does data directly support the claims? Correlation is not causation.

### R: Reason
Is the logical chain sound? No gaps, no leaps, no circular reasoning.

### I: Intent
Is the output aligned with the stated goal? No scope creep.

### T: Truth
Is this factually accurate? No bias, no selective omission.

### A: Adapt
If new information contradicts prior claims, update the output. Do not defend errors.

### S: Synthesis
Does the whole output hold together as a coherent, consistent argument?

---

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] V: Sources verified and cited.
- [ ] E: Evidence directly supports each claim.
- [ ] R: Logic chain is complete and sound.
- [ ] I: Output serves stated intent.
- [ ] T: No factual errors or omissions found.
- [ ] A: Updated where new information conflicts.
- [ ] S: Output is internally consistent.

All seven pass: proceed to guardrails.
Any fail: revise that section, re-run VERITAS.
